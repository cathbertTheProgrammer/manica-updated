Our Team

We are a group of innovative, experienced, and proficient teams. You will love to collaborate with us.


Mrs. Patricia Lees-Rolfe

Chief Executive Officer

Qualifications

M Ed (University of Sheffield), BSc (University of Natal) and HDE (University of Cape Town)

Biography Mrs Patricia Lees-Rolfe has taught Sciences and Mathematics for over 40 years. She has worked in South Africa, Singapore and Malawi and has taught in 5 different educational systems. This has enabled her to adapt to different requirements and to understand the procedural exercises that need to be followed for each one. She has been a HOD in 2 of the school systems, so has further understanding of processes.
Dr. Clemence Marimo

Vice Chancellor

Qualifications

BChD (AUT, Gr); MChD ORAL PATH (UWC, SA); FAIMER Fellow

Biography Dr. Marimo is a well renowned medical professional who has over twenty-five years of experience in both medical practice and academia across Africa and beyond African boundaries. He has served at top management level of both research and academic institutions which include; School of Dentistry, College of Medicine & Health Sciences, University of Rwanda, Cavendish University Zambia, University of Zambia [UNZA] School of Medicine, Examinations Council of Health Sciences (ECOHS) – Department of Medical Education Development,. He has also served on boards and committees which include; Higher Education Authority of Zambia (2022), Health Professions Council of Zambia Investigations Committee (2022), Interim Chair, Research Group of the International Academy of Physician Associate Educators [IAPAE] (2018), Principal Consultant and Operational Chair; and Member of the Education and Liaison Committee of the Medical and Dental Practitioners Council of Zimbabwe (2008).
Dr. Muchenelah Chibasa

Dean School of Medicine and Health Sciences

Qualifications

MBChB (2006-2009), University of Zambia.
BSc Human Biology (2004-2006), University of Zambia.
Bachelor of Science in Molecular Biology and Physiological Science and Chemistry (2000-2003), University of Namibia.

Biography Dr. Muchenelah Chibasa is a highly driven and accomplished professional with over a decade of experience at senior management levels, particularly in the realm of Health Regulation. Her career trajectory reflects a commitment to excellence, as evidenced by her tenure at the Health Professions Council of Zambia, where she navigated roles ranging from Director Inspectorate to Assistant Registrar-Training, ensuring compliance with health standards and training of health professionals thereby facilitating quality healthcare delivery. Her leadership extends beyond the confines of her workplace, contributing to various national committees and nonprofit organizations aimed at enhancing healthcare access and quality. In all her pursuits, Dr. Chibasa continues to exemplify dedication to her profession, combining expertise with a compassionate approach to improving health outcomes for communities.

Qualifications

Biography Mrs Sarah S. Tembo is a Marketing Strategist with over ten years wealth of experience.
